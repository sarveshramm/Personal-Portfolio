Eduntra AI

Personalized Learning & Career Guidance for Every Student

An inclusive AI-powered education and career guidance platform built for rural, low-income, and underserved communities with offline access, multi-language support, and real-time insights.

Project Description

Eduntra AI is an inclusive AI-powered education and career guidance platform built for rural, low-income, and underserved communities. It provides personalized learning support, multi-language explanations, voice interactions, offline AI tutoring, real-time progress analytics, and early career discovery with guided roadmaps.

Problem Statement

Millions of students, especially in rural and low-income regions, struggle with:

  • Limited access to qualified teachers
  • Language barriers and inaccessible learning materials
  • Lack of personalized academic support
  • Poor internet connectivity & digital divide
  • Overcrowded classrooms with minimal one-on-one attention
  • No guidance for future career opportunities
  • Barriers to inclusion for differently-abled learners

Technologies Used

Next.js React.js Tailwind CSS AI/ML APIs LocalStorage JavaScript HTML5 CSS3 NLP Engines

Key Features

Dual Login System

Teacher & student logins with personalized dashboards, progress tracking, and admin analytics.

Offline AI Tutor

Works with limited or no internet. Supports English & regional languages with visual explanations.

Personalized Learning Path

AI identifies weak areas and recommends study plans, micro-lessons & adaptive quizzes.

Career Path Finder

Suggests suitable careers based on strengths & interests with step-by-step roadmaps.

Teacher Dashboard

Class & individual progress overview with AI-powered insights for targeted interventions.

Multi-language Support

Supports 19,000+ languages with voice interactions and localized explanations.

Impact & Vision

Impact

  • Bridges learning inequality in underserved communities
  • Powers early career guidance for informed choices
  • Enables offline education beyond internet barriers
  • Scalable for government schools, NGOs & rural centers

Vision

To make quality education & career guidance accessible to every learner — regardless of language, location, or background — through adaptive and inclusive AI.
